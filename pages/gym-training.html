Gym Training

Add 2 to 3 Compound Exercises and 1 to 2 Isolation Exercises

FREITAS, Victor. Several Rogue Gym Plates. 2018.

Beginner

Day Activation Compound I Compound II Isolation I Isolation II Light Cardio
1. Pull Active Hang Lat Pulldown Seated Cable Row Hammer Curl Cable Crunch Bike
2. Rest
3. Push Ring Support Hold Assisted Dip Chest Press Tricep Extension Cable Shoulder External Rotation Bike
4. Rest
5. Leg Wall Sit Squat Romanian Deadlift Seated Calf Raise Standing Calf Raise Bike
6. Rest

Man

Day Activation Compound I Compound II Isolation I Isolation II Light Cardio
1. Pull Active Hang Weighted Pull-Up Bent-Over Row Hammer Curl Wrist Curl Bike
2. Shoulder Feet-Elevated Pike Hold Overhead Shoulder Press Face Pull Cable Side Lateral Raise Shrug Bike
3. Rest
4. Push Ring Support Hold Weighted Dip Incline Bench Press Tricep Extension Cable Shoulder External Rotation Bike
5. Leg Wall Sit Squat Deadlift Seated Calf Raise Standing Calf Raise Bike
6. Rest

Woman

Day Activation Compound I Compound II Isolation I Isolation II Light Cardio
1. Push Ring Support Hold Dip Chest Press Tricep Extension Cable Shoulder External Rotation Bike
2. Leg I Good Morning Hold Romanian Deadlift Lunge Cable Hip Abduction Cable Hip Adduction Bike
3. Rest
4. Pull Active Hang Lat Pulldown Seated Cable Row Hammer Curl Cable Crunch Bike
5. Leg II Wall Sit Squat Hip Thrust Seated Calf Raise Standing Calf Raise Bike
6. Rest

Phases

  • Activation: 1 set of 15 s of isometric hold with moderate tension. Rest 30 s.
  • Compound I/II: 2 sets of 5–7 reps at an explosive pace with progressive overload1 at 0–1 RIR. Rest 5 min.
  • Isolation I/II: 3 sets of 10–12 reps at an explosive pace with progressive overload at 0–1 RIR. Rest 3 min.
  • Light Cardio: 10 minutes at low intensity (zone 1).

  1. Progressive overload: In the last set, if you feel you could have done more reps than the target RIR, increase the load by 2.5 to 5 kg in the next session.↩︎
