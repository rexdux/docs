Instructions

  • Activation: 2 isometric sets of 15–20 s at max tension. Rest 30 s after each set.
  • Compound: 2 sets of 5–7 reps to failure. Rest 5 min after each set.
  • Isolation: 3 sets of 10–12 reps with 1–2 RIR. Rest 3 min after each set.
  • Skill Unlock / High-Intensity Cardio: 10–15 min.
